A Little Bit About Me

I am a 21 year old interaction designer and student from the Big B, (Belfast, NI). I like to dabble in all shapes and sizes of design and the creative industry, I am currently studying IXD at Ulster University that I am really enjoying as I am constantly learning about new aspects of the industry that I definently see myself being a part of as I continue develope my skills. I have recently taken a shine to videography I find it so much fun and it entertains me for hours!

I have many interests such as music and fashion but mostly I enjoy creating new things that hopefully other people will like aswell, I hope this passion shows thoughout my work. I also have a huge passion for travelling, I 100% have the travel bug, I love is to indulge myself within different cultures, experience new things and make lots of memories.
